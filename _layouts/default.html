Rajdeep Singh

I am a MS Robotics student at the GRASP Lab, part of the Penn Engineering at University of Pennsylvania. Most recently, I was a research assistant for Prof. Antonio Loquercio during my first semester..

From 2024 to 2025, I was a research assistant in DREAM Lab at Indian Institute of Science advised by Prof. Yogesh Simmhan.

In my past life, from 2018 to 2023, I worked as a SDE-3 at Loco, which was one of India's biggest esports platform. I primarily built ML infrastructure, data pipelines and backend services to handle milions of daily active users.
